---

## 🌟 Overview

Rite of Renaissance is an experimental project exploring the intersection of:
- **Existential philosophy** (meaning-making, ambiguity tolerance)
- **Contemplative practice** (structured introspection, symbolic rituals)
- **Digital tools** (interactive simulations, guided protocols)

The project offers accessible protocols for individuals seeking to develop resilience in the face of uncertainty, complexity, and the unknown.

---

## 🎯 Core Components

### 1. **Safe Aperture Simulation**
An interactive web-based training program for developing ambiguity tolerance through graduated exposure.

**🔗 Access:** [Safe Aperture Platform](https://riteofrenaissance.github.io/safe-aperture-study/)

**Features:**
- 5 progressive levels (everyday → existential ambiguity)
- Built-in safety mechanisms (Emergency Exit, grounding techniques)
- Self-paced, accessible anywhere
- No login required

**Use Cases:**
- Personal development
- Anxiety management
- Decision-making under uncertainty
- Philosophical inquiry

---

### 2. **Philosophical Framework**
Conceptual foundations drawn from:
- Phenomenology (Heidegger, Merleau-Ponty)
- Existentialism (Sartre, Camus)
- Pragmatism (James, Dewey)
- Contemplative traditions (Buddhist mindfulness, Stoic practice)

**Key Concepts:**
- **Ambiguity as generative** (not merely problematic)
- **Understanding as practice** (not possession)
- **Meaning as emergent** (not pre-given)

---

### 3. **Ritual Protocols**
Structured practices for engaging with uncertainty:
- Sovereign Initiation (self-determination ritual)
- Meaning Cartography (personal value mapping)
- Existential Inquiry (guided questioning)
